## Personal Change
As the fall arrives, the most awaited event of the year "AWS Summit Toronto 2025" concluded on September 4, 2025 in Metro Toronto Convention Centre - South Building.  Compared to the previous year, where I had flown from Northern Ontario, just to attend this event, this year I had relocated to Toronto to expose myself to the community and expand my networks. Thus, I had a great opportunity to attend multiple partner events as well as spend a full day at the summit. 

## Volunteering at AWS Community Lounge
![AWS Certification](/images/blog-images/aws-summit/aws_summit_venue_2025.jpeg)
Artificial Intelligence has significantly changed the landscape of software development and everybody has shown interest in either learning or implementing them. With my experience of giving AWS AI Practitioner exam and my ongoing preparation for AWS DevOps - Professional exam, I was very excited and eager to volunteer at AWS Community Lounge.

Volunteering at AWS Community Lounge allowed me to catch up with my fellow community builders and also gave me the opportunity to interact in-person with the enthusiast visitors. My objective was to provide correct guidance for AWS certifications, community builders program and share about the essential development tools within the AWS ecosystem. Visitors were interested in learning about AWS AI services and interacting with them enhanced my own understanding of this domain. 

## AWS Community Builders After-Party
After a long day, I attended AWS Community Builder After-Party along with all the fellow community builders and leaders. The day ended with a really good dinner and a lot of casual interactions about growth as well as opportunities.
![AWS Certification](/images/blog-images/aws-summit/cb_mixer_party.PNG)

## Looking Forward to 2026
In 2025, I had accomplished my goal of upskilling myself with AI foundations and completing my AWS Certifications. I have been continuously putting effort to share my knowledge through different blogging sites including my own personal blogging website. Now, my next milestone is to create video contents in tutorial format on AWS Services and distribute through the YouTube Channel.
