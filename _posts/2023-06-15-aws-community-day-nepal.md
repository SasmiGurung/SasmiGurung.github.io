I am thrilled to share my first ever experience as a speaker with you all after experiencing the amazing opportunity to give a talk at the AWS Community Day 2022 organized by the AWS User Group Nepal. As a passionate AWS enthusiast with hands-on experience on various projects, being invited to speak at such a grand event was a true honor.

When I received the invitation to speak, I became very excited and started my preparation immediately. I carefully selected the topic that I was interested in and spent several hours researching, organizing, and refining my content for the event. Although the preparation phase was daunting and I became very anxious as I was going to speak for the first time, it pushed me to learn deeply about the topic. When the day of the event arrived, excitement and nervousness flowed through me. The event setup was very grand and all the participants were full of energy.

![introduction](/images/blog-images/aws-community-day-nepal/introduction.jpg)

I took a deep breath as I entered the stage and started my presentation. I immediately captured the audience’s attention with a fascinating introduction. I focused on sharing practical examples and real-world use cases with a demonstration, ensuring that attendees could apply the concepts in their own AWS projects. This approach really helped me to maintain the attention of the audience towards the session.

After my presentation, attendees had the chance to learn more about the topic during the QA Session. The questions from attendees were very interesting and it showed how passionate the audience was about AWS. I tried to cover all the questions from the audience based on my experience and knowledge. Another major highlight of the event was the chance to connect with fellow AWS enthusiasts and speakers. A valuable part of the day was having conversations, exchanging ideas and sharing experiences with them. I also made new connections and their cloud journey was very inspiring.

This speaking opportunity was not just a chance to share my knowledge but also a life-changing experience for me. It made me realize the importance of community driven events like AWS Community Day in promoting learning and growth. The interaction with energetic attendees and experienced people from the industry provided me the fresh perspectives on AWS technologies and its best practices.

![token-of-appreciation](/images/blog-images/aws-community-day-nepal/token-of-appreciation.jpg)

Being a speaker at the AWS Community Day was an unforgettable experience. I am grateful for the opportunity to share my insights and contribute to the AWS Community. I look forward to future opportunities to engage with this energetic community and continue sharing my passion for AWS.
