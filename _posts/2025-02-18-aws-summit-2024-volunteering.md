## The Journey
The **AWS Summit Toronto 2024** was organized on September 11, 2024 at Metro Toronto Convention Centre — South Building. The event was fully booked and the online registration closed early because of overbooking. I flew from Northern Ontario, specifically for this event and I almost missed the registration. The venue was well planned and different sessions were fully seated. Coming from Nepal where I attended and gave a session at **AWS Community Day Nepal** in 2023, the scale of this event was truly mesmerizing.


## AWS Community Lounge

![group](/images/blog-images/summit-2024/group.jpg)

<p style="text-align: center;">AWS Community Lounge</p>

As an AWS Community Builder, I had the opportunity to volunteer at the **AWS Community Lounge**, where community builders gathered to share insights with visitors and learners. It was an incredible experience engaging with students, professionals, and AWS enthusiasts, answering questions about cloud computing, AWS certifications, and best practices.

One of the highlights was meeting my regional community manager, **[Jason Dunn](linkedin.com/in/jasonrobertdunn)**, and having an insightful conversation about AWS communities and career growth. I also connected with AWS employees and engineers from AWS USA, who showcased cutting-edge innovations in AI, serverless computing, and security. Their passion for technology was truly inspiring.

## AWS Community Builders After-Party
The day ended on a high note with an exclusive after-party for AWS Community Builders. It was a fantastic opportunity to network, share experiences, and celebrate the AWS community. The energy and enthusiasm from AWS staff were unmatched, reinforcing how AWS values and supports its community.

## Word of Advice
The AWS Summit is a really big event drawing many visitors, students, professionals and business due to which the sponsors have to limit the passes for the event. I found that if the limited quota for the event is full, then the online registration is closed early. So, if you are travelling from a different city just like me, I would suggest registering early when the online registration opens. Alternatively, you can try registering on-site at the venue by speaking with the event staff, but passes are not always guaranteed.

## My Plan for 2025
In 2024, I attended the **New Voice** program for Community Builder, which was communication training for public speaking and AWS events. Unfortunately, as I was living in Northern Ontario, I was not able to fully utilise the learning from my training into actual events. So, my plan for 2025, is to move to Toronto and capitalise the AWS Toronto User Group to expand my network and be the part of different user group events. I aim to give a session at a big summit and advocate for AWS.
